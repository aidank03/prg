About

The Earlham Plasma Research Group (PRG) is a research group at Earlham College in Richmond, IN. Using Earlham's Advanced Physics Laboratory, students may conduct experiments under high vacuum using a variety of resources. While experiments may be conducted in a wide range of fields, the PRG focuses on low energy plasmasa and plasma applications to controlled fusion research.

The purpose of the PRG website is to direct interested students to Earlham's PRG lab and to provide a collection of resources for students to learn Fusion Sciences on their own.
